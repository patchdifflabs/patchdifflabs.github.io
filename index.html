About

PatchDiff Labs is an independent security research lab focused on deep vulnerability research, patch diffing, reverse engineering, and zero-day risk analysis.

We study why vulnerabilities exist, how fixes behave, and whether residual or variant risk remains after patching.

Research Focus

  • Patch diffing & variant analysis
  • Root-cause analysis of critical CVEs
  • Exploitability & residual risk assessment
  • Responsible disclosure & vendor coordination
